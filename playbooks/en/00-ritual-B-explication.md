# Playbook 00 — Ritual B: Explication (positioning + confirmation)

> **When to run:** immediately after Ritual A.
> **Language:** everything the user sees is in the **active locale** (this file: English).
> **Goal:** turn answers + materials into concrete positioning, return it to the user **in their own words**, and obtain **explicit confirmation**. No other playbook runs before confirmation.

---

## 0. The prime rule

> **No actions (playbooks 01–07) without explicit user confirmation of positioning.**

If not confirmed — refine, rewrite, show again. Don't make "in their best interest" assumptions.

---

## 1. Inputs

- `profile/intake-answers.md` — intake answers (quotes).
- `profile/materials/` — resume, cases, links.
- The "⚠️ Contradictions" block — open questions from Ritual A.

## 2. Output

One file: **`profile/positioning.md`**, structured below. This is the single source of truth for all other playbooks.

## 3. Structure of `positioning.md`

```md
# Positioning (draft v1 — for confirmation)

## 1. Positioning statement (one-two lines)
[Who you are + for whom + what's unique. E.g., "Product Design Lead specializing in … .
 Looking for teams that …"]

## 2. Craft ↔ strategy axis (where you want to be)
## 3. People vs craft; team scale
## 4. Mission — filter or desirable; what "helping people" means to you
## 5. Must-have (required)
## 6. No-go (definitely not)
## 7. Comp floor / currency / format
## 8. Geography and relocation/remote readiness
## 9. Target company archetypes (with examples)
## 10. Inbound channels (have / building)
## 11. 2–3 anchor cases (role / process / outcome / metrics) — core of content
## 12. Alignment-screening criteria (for playbook 02) — a yes/no checklist
## 13. Open questions (unresolved contradictions)
```

---

## 4. Procedure

1. **Synthesize** `positioning.md` strictly from answers + materials. Quote the user where it strengthens (sections 4, 5, 6).
2. **Do not invent** a domain or industry. If the user didn't name one — leave section 4 honestly open ("direction TBD"); do not default to "psychology" or similar.
3. **Resolve/surface contradictions:** for each item in "⚠️ Contradictions" either propose a resolution (with a question) or log it as open in section 13.
4. **Build the alignment-screen checklist** (section 12) — concrete criteria by which playbook 02 will score inbound opportunities (mission, design maturity, autonomy, comp, geography).
5. **Show the user** a concise summary of `positioning.md` (not the whole file — a digest of 1 + 5 + 6 + 7 + 12) and ask: "Is this right? What to adjust?"

---

## 5. Confirmation gate

- Ask explicitly: **"Do you confirm this positioning as the basis for our work?"**
- If **yes**:
  1. Write the final version to `profile/positioning.md` (drop the "draft / for confirmation" tag).
  2. Create `profile/materials-present.md` with the date and a short ready note.
  3. Say the system is in **ready state** and offer the first **weekly digest** (playbook `07-weekly-digest`).
- If **no / refine**: return to step 1 with specific edits. Loop until an explicit "yes."

---

## 6. Do NOT

- Run playbooks 01–06 before confirmation.
- Insert "psychology/healthtech" or any domain the user didn't name.
- Set salary or geography yourself — only from the user's answers.
- Show non-active-locale output to the user.
